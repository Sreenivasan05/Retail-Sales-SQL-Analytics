## Key Insights from Retail Sales Analysis

### Overall Business Performance

- The business generated ₹6.18M in total sales with ₹1.61M total profit, resulting in an overall profit margin of ~26%
- Total quantity sold across all orders was 13K units, indicating healthy transaction volume

**Insight:**
The company operates with strong profitability, suggesting effective pricing and cost control across categories.

### Category-Level Performance

- Office Supplies emerged as the top-selling category with ₹2.09M, closely followed by Electronics (₹2.05M) and Furniture (₹2.04M)
- Profit distribution across categories is well-balanced, with no single category dominating profits.

**Insight:**
Sales and profits are diversified across categories, reducing dependency risk on any single product line.

### Sub-Category Profitability

- High-performing sub-categories include:

    - Paper & Markers (Office Supplies)

    - Chairs & Tables (Furniture)

    - Printers & Electronic Games (Electronics)

- Several sub-categories show profit margins exceeding 140%, indicating strong pricing power.

**Insight:**
Certain sub-categories contribute disproportionately high profit, making them prime candidates for focused promotions and inventory prioritization.

### Sales & Profit Trends Over Time

- Sales show a steady upward trend over the years, with recent years outperforming earlier periods
- Monthly sales exhibit seasonal fluctuations, indicating demand variability.
- Cumulative sales consistently increase over time, confirming sustained business growth.

**Insight:**
The business demonstrates long-term growth with seasonal peaks, which can be leveraged for campaign planning.

### Customer Behavior Analysis

- Repeat customers account for ~58% of the customer base, while 42% are one-time buyers
- The top 10 customers contribute a significant share of total revenue, each generating ~₹24K–₹29K in sales.

**Insight:**
Customer retention is strong, but there is opportunity to convert one-time customers into repeat buyers through loyalty programs.

### Geographic Performance

- Top-performing states include New York, Florida, and California, each generating over ₹1M in sales
- Some cities show high sales but relatively low profit margins, indicating potential cost or discounting issues.

**Insight:**
Geographic optimization is needed — especially in high-sales, low-profit cities — to improve margin efficiency.

### Payment Mode Preferences

- Debit Cards and Credit Cards are the most preferred payment methods, followed closely by UPI
- COD and EMI contribute less but still represent meaningful revenue streams.

**Insight:**
Digital payment modes dominate transactions, highlighting the importance of seamless digital checkout experiences.


## Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on high-margin sub-categories.

- Improve profitability in high-sales but low-margin cities through pricing or logistics optimization.

- Introduce retention strategies to increase repeat customer percentage.

- Leverage seasonal trends for targeted promotional campaigns.
